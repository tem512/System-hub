The Ethiopian Statistics Service (ESS) is the national statistical Service responsible for collecting, analyzing, and disseminating official statistical data. ESS was established in [1959] to provide accurate and reliable statistical information to support evidence-based decision-making and policy formulation in the country. It operates under the Ministry of Planning and Development Minister, which oversees its activities and ensures coordination with other government agencies.

The Ethiopian Statistics Service has many divisions. Among those divisions, the Gender Department stands out with its array of responsibilities. One crucial aspect of its mandate is overseeing the organizational Child daycare facilities. Recognizing the importance of efficient management in fulfilling its duties, the Gender Department has identified the need for an automated system to streamline child daycare operations. This automation aims to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and overall effectiveness in managing daycare services within the organization.

Understanding the intricacies involved in developing such a system, the Gender Department has formally approached the IT department for collaboration. This collaboration entails a comprehensive approach, encompassing the design, implementation, and testing phases of the proposed daycare management system.
