The Ethiopian Statistics Service (ESS) is the national statistical Service responsible for collecting, analyzing, and disseminating official statistical data. ESS was established in [1959] to provide accurate and reliable statistical information to support evidence-based decision-making and policy formulation in the country. It operates under the Ministry of Planning and Development Minister, which oversees its activities and ensures coordination with other government agencies. The Ethiopian Statistics Service has many divisions.
